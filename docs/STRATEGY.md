# HarvardTax — Strategic Roadmap

*Last updated: February 25, 2026*

## Vision

**The first all-in-one Tax Strategy + Tax Structure + Tax Filing service for partnerships and LLCs.**

CPA accuracy. Attorney-grade structuring. AI speed. One roof.

## The Job To Be Done

> "Help me pay the least amount of tax legally possible, now and in the future, without me having to become a tax expert."

Today this job is fragmented across 3-4 providers who don't talk to each other:
- CPA → files returns (backward-looking)
- Tax attorney → sets up structures (forward-looking)
- Financial advisor → investment strategy
- Estate planner → trusts, succession

The client coordinates. Nobody sees the full picture. Money falls through the cracks.

**HarvardTax becomes the single provider that sees everything.**

## The Pain (Paxology Lens)

The surface pain: "Filing my 1065 is expensive and slow."

The deeper pain:
- "I don't know if my business structure is costing me money"
- "My CPA files but never proactively suggests changes"
- "I've heard about trusts, S-corps, QSBs — nobody explains it plainly"
- "I pay 3 professionals who each see one piece of my puzzle"
- "I'm reactive on taxes instead of strategic"

## Phased Rollout

### Phase 1: The Wedge (Now — Q1 2026)
**Partnership Tax Filing Service**

- File Form 1065 + K-1s for partnerships/LLCs
- AI prepares, CPA reviews
- Flat-fee pricing: $499 / $799 / $1,499
- QuickBooks integration for automated data pull
- **Goal:** First 10 paying clients. Prove the model works.

This is concrete, sellable, deliverable today. Every client becomes a lead for Phase 2.

### Phase 2: The Upsell (Months 2-3)
**Tax Structure Review**

- Add a "Tax Structure Review" as a paid add-on ($299-499)
- Analyze client's current entity structure against alternatives
- Deliverable: Written report with recommendations (e.g., "You could save $X/year by electing S-corp status")
- This is consulting/advisory, not legal advice — no attorney required yet
- Natural upsell from every 1065 filing: "We noticed your structure may not be optimal..."

**Revenue model:** Every $499 filing client sees a $299 upsell. 30% conversion = +$90 effective ACV.

### Phase 3: The Attorney (Months 3-6)
**Entity Structuring & Trust Formation**

- Partner with or bring on a tax attorney (1099 or equity)
- Add services: trust formation, entity restructuring, S-corp elections, QSBS planning
- Pricing: $1,500-5,000 per engagement
- **Key hire:** Tax attorney who wants to build, not bill hourly

Services to add:
- Revocable living trusts
- Irrevocable trusts (asset protection, estate)
- LLC → S-corp election advisory + execution
- Multi-entity structuring (holding co + operating co)
- QSBS (Qualified Small Business Stock) planning

### Phase 4: The Platform (Months 6-12)
**All-In-One Tax Intelligence**

- Rebrand/expand positioning: "Tax Strategy. Tax Structure. Tax Filing."
- Annual relationship: structure review + annual filing + ongoing advisory
- Client dashboard: see all entities, filings, structure, optimization opportunities
- AI proactively identifies tax-saving opportunities from client data
- Recurring revenue: annual filing + advisory retainer ($2,000-5,000/year per client)

### Phase 5: Scale (Year 2+)
**Digital Products + Content Funnel**

- Educational guides on Gumroad ($29-97): "Partnership Tax Returns Decoded", "How to Structure Your LLC", "Building a Trust"
- Free content (blog, social, YouTube) drives top-of-funnel
- Guides convert cold traffic → warm leads → service clients
- Info products fund marketing spend
- Community/membership for ongoing tax Q&A

## The AI Moat

Every phase deepens the AI advantage:
- Phase 1: QBO → 1065 mapping engine (40+ account types)
- Phase 2: Structure analysis engine (compare entity types, model tax outcomes)
- Phase 3: Trust/entity document generation (AI drafts, attorney reviews)
- Phase 4: Proactive tax optimization from longitudinal client data
- Phase 5: Content generation from real anonymized case studies

The more entity types and structures we handle, the better the AI gets. Competitors would need to rebuild this from scratch.

## Key Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Need a CPA to review returns | Dar's network; contract CPA initially ($50-100/return) |
| Tax attorney required for Phase 3 | Start with referral partnerships, convert to hire/equity |
| Malpractice liability | E&O insurance; clear engagement letters; attorney signs off on legal work |
| Market timing (tax season) | Launch filing service before March 15 deadline; off-season focus on structure work |
| Regulatory (UPL concerns) | Clear separation: CPA work vs. attorney work; proper licensing |
