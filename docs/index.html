Frequently Asked Questions

Does HarvardTax replace my tax software?

No — HarvardTax generates filled IRS Form 1065 and K-1 PDFs. You can review them, file directly, or import into your existing e-file software. We eliminate the data entry, not your workflow.

What if a QBO account doesn't map correctly?

You have full override control. After generation, review every mapping — if something should go to a different 1065 line, change it with one click and regenerate instantly.

Is my client data secure?

Yes. We use read-only QBO access via OAuth2 (we never see your QBO password). Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. SSNs are used only for PDF generation and not stored permanently.

What types of partnerships are supported?

LLCs, LPs, LLPs, and general partnerships. We handle pro-rata allocations now, with waterfall and preferred return support coming soon.

How much does it cost?

Free during early access. We'll introduce pricing tiers as we approach general availability — pricing will be per-return, significantly less than the time cost of manual preparation.
