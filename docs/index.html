How It Works

From fund docs to K‑1 distribution in four steps.

1

Share Your Fund Documents

Upload your LPA, subscription agreements, cap table, and financial statements. Connect QuickBooks if you have it — or just send us what you've got. Our AI parses the operating agreement to extract allocation rules, partner details, and fund terms automatically.

2

We Value Your Portfolio (ASC 820)

Our valuation team — with 1,000+ valuations and Big Four pedigree — values each portfolio company using ASC 820-compliant methodologies. Latest round, OPM backsolve, DCF, market comps — whatever fits. Audit-ready documentation included.

3

Valuations Flow Into Your Tax Return

Here's the magic: your portfolio valuations feed directly into the 1065 — unrealized gains/losses, capital account adjustments, Schedule L, M‑1, M‑2. No re-keying, no handoff, no reconciliation gaps. A licensed CPA reviews everything and e-files.

4

K‑1s Delivered to Every LP

Each LP gets their individual K‑1 with properly allocated income, gains, losses, and deductions — all consistent with the valuations. Distribute via our portal, email, or download the full package.

Frequently Asked Questions

How are your valuations different from my fund admin's?

Most fund admins don't do valuations at all — they refer out to third-party firms. Those firms produce a standalone report that then has to be manually integrated into your tax return. We do both, so the valuations flow directly into K‑1 allocations with zero manual handoff.

Are your ASC 820 valuations audit-ready?

Yes. Our team has delivered 1,000+ valuations with a 100% audit success rate. We provide full documentation including methodology selection, comparable analysis, discount rates, and fair value hierarchy classification — ready for Big Four review.

What if I already have a fund admin?

We complement your fund admin, not replace them. Your admin handles capital calls, distributions, and LP communications. We handle the annual compliance that most admins don't — portfolio valuations and the tax return. Many fund admins refer these services out anyway.

What if I just need a tax return, not valuations?

Absolutely. We prepare standalone 1065 returns and K‑1s for LLCs, partnerships, and funds starting at $499. The bundle pricing is for funds that need both valuations and tax — but you can buy either service independently.

Can you handle our fund's deadline?

Partnership returns are due March 15 (or September 15 with extension). We can file Form 7004 extensions and work with your timeline. For new engagements starting close to the deadline, we'll file the extension first and then complete the return with the care it deserves.

Do you replace my fund administrator?

No. HarvardTax handles annual compliance (valuations + tax). Your fund admin handles ongoing operations — capital calls, distributions, LP onboarding, NAV reporting. We work alongside your admin, not instead of them. If you don't have an admin, we can recommend one.
