Frequently Asked Questions

Who actually prepares and reviews my return?

Our AI technology handles the data extraction, account mapping, and initial preparation. Then a licensed CPA reviews every line of the return for accuracy, compliance, and edge cases before delivering it to you. You get the speed of AI with the judgment of an experienced tax professional.

How does flat-fee pricing work?

You get a fixed price quote before we start — based on your partnership's complexity (number of partners, allocation structure, etc.). That's what you pay. Period. No hourly billing, no surprise invoices, no scope creep charges.

What if I don't use QuickBooks?

QuickBooks Online gives us the fastest turnaround because we can pull data automatically. If you use a different accounting system, we can still help — just send us your trial balance and financial statements. Turnaround may be slightly longer.

What types of partnerships do you handle?

LLCs taxed as partnerships, limited partnerships (LPs), LLPs, and general partnerships. We handle pro-rata allocations, special allocations, waterfall structures, preferred returns, and carried interest.

How fast will I get my return?

Simple partnerships (2-3 members, pro-rata): 24-48 hours. Complex structures: 3-5 business days. During peak tax season, add a day or two. We'll give you an estimated delivery date with your quote.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. We use read-only OAuth2 access to QuickBooks (we can never modify your books). All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. SSNs are used only for K-1 preparation and are not stored after delivery.

What's included in the accuracy guarantee?

If a return we prepared contains an error that results in an IRS penalty, we'll cover the penalty amount and re-prepare the return at no additional cost. This applies to all returns reviewed and delivered by HarvardTax.
