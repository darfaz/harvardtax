Frequently Asked Questions

I'm a business owner — do I need to know anything about taxes?

No. Connect your QuickBooks and upload your operating agreement. Our AI handles the mapping and a licensed CPA reviews and files everything. You just get your K-1s.

I'm a CPA — does this replace Drake?

No. HarvardTax sits between QuickBooks and Drake. We automate the mapping and allocation, then export a trial balance file that imports directly into Drake. You still review and e-file in Drake.

What if a mapping is wrong?

Every mapping is visible and overridable. Change any QBO → 1065 line mapping with one click and regenerate. For filing clients, your CPA catches these in review. For platform users, you're in full control.

What types of partnerships are supported?

LLCs, LPs, LLPs, general partnerships, and fund/SPV structures. Pro-rata allocations now, with waterfall, preferred return, and carried interest support coming soon.

Is my data secure?

Read-only QBO access via OAuth2. Data encrypted in transit and at rest. SSNs used only for K-1 generation and not stored permanently.

What about Lacerte or CCH ProSystem?

Drake is our first integration — the most popular tax software among small and mid-size firms. Lacerte and CCH support are on the roadmap.
